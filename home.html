About Abhivriddhi - Student Training & Development Committee

Abhivriddhi is the Student Training & Development Committee focused on nurturing skills, fostering personal growth, and empowering students to make a positive impact. We believe in creating opportunities for students to enhance their leadership and professional skills through various activities, workshops, and events.

Our key areas of focus include:

  • Leadership development programs
  • Soft skills and communication training
  • Career readiness workshops
  • Networking events with industry experts

We aim to foster growth in all students and prepare them for the challenges of the professional world.
